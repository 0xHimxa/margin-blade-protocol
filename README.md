### The Survival Stakes Arena

**Margin Blade** is a decentralized RPG built on Ethereum. It introduces the concept of **Collateralized Gaming**, where every Hero NFT is tied to a financial position in **$EDGE** (a stablecoin backed by ETH). 

Battle for rewards, overdrive your stats, and manage your health factor to avoid liquidation in the arena.
